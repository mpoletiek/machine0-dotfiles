# 🚀 Neovim Mastery Tutorial

Welcome to your new VS Code-killer setup! This guide is interactive—try the commands as you read.

## 1. Finding Your Way Around
*   **File Explorer**: Press `<leader>e` to focus the tree on the left. Use `j/k` to move and `Enter` to open.
*   **Fuzzy Finder**: Press `<leader>ff` to find files by name, or `<leader>fg` to search for text inside files.
*   **Command Palette**: Forget a shortcut? Press `Ctrl + p` to search all commands.

## 2. Coding Like a Pro (LSP)
Your LSPs (Pyright, vtsls, Solidity) are active.
*   **Go to Definition**: Put your cursor on a variable and press `gd`.
*   **Hover Docs**: Press `K` to see documentation for the item under your cursor.
*   **Rename**: Press `<leader>rn` to rename a symbol across the whole project.
*   **Code Actions**: Press `<leader>ca` for "Quick Fixes" (like adding missing imports).

## 3. Formatting & Linting
*   **Auto-Format**: Every time you save a JS, TS, or Solidity file, **Prettier** runs automatically.
*   **Manual Format**: Press `<leader>f` at any time.
*   **Problems**: Press `<leader>xx` to toggle the "Trouble" list of all errors in your code.

## 4. Mastery Tip
Whenever you start a command with `<leader>`, wait one second. A window will pop up showing you every possible key you can press next. This is the best way to learn!
